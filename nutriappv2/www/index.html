THE COMPANY

NutriTECH Alliance Corporation is the premier distributor of EXCLUSIVE Quality Home Living Premium products in the Philippines with a complete product line up that promotes healthy living. It is dedicated to increase the people's awareness on their health and well-being while attending to their basic needs i.e., Food, Water and Air.

NutriTECH is a two word composition: Nutrition and TECHnology. It promotes Good Nutrition through proper Food Preparation using the latest, up-to-date and innovative Technology where our products are made of. NutriTECH was registered formally in the year 2003. its Head Office and Showroom are located at the Ortigas Business Center, Pasig City. It also owned a warehouse, a depot of products for storage and with lodging facilities for out-of-town NutriTECHians. It also gives its members a FREE team building and leadership meeting at Executive Wellspring Resort and at Tagaytay Highlands Karlstate Logwood Homes, a "First Class Training Camp". All of these are greatly part of building a stronger NutriTECH family.

Today NutriTECH continuously creates unlimited opportunities for everyone using a wide and stable system of Direct Selling Business. We offer unique and perfectly patterned SSTM Program where it means progress and reward for every member. NutriTECH has improved the quality of life of its member. NutriTECH gives allowances and incentives on top of the benefits our Manager received. FREE Travel Lifestyle every year to exciting destinations here and abroad. Health Benefit, Scholarship Program people - members and administration.


VISION

Presently, we are the country's leading Company in an Exclusive Quality Home Living Premium Products Industry. This is so because we are equipped with Dedicated and Efficient Sales Team, Executives, Managers and Staff as well as Dynamic System and Technology which enable us to continue providing our Customers with world-class Products and Excellent Services. As such, we expect continuous growth and to remain on top not only today but in the days ahead of us.


MISSION

We will use commonly shared business ethics and values as a guide in attaining our goal for profit and excellence. These time-held values will serve as a constant reminder for all of us be fair, just and honorable in all our dealings and business transactions.

We have faith in our products. We strongly believe in the capability of our products to be of great importance to the Filipino family's health and well being in which we will provide NutriTION while making use of the LATEST TECHNOLOGY.

We will conduct our business professionally. We will respect our business partners and hold our potential clients in high esteem. Every person will be regarded as a potential business partner who can propel the company forward. We will attain profitability through progress and teamwork. People working cooperatively as a team with a fixed goal in mind accomplishes more than scattered individuals working independently in random. We will prosper as we work and achieve expertise as we endeavor.

We will strive for excellence. We will work with only the best in mind. We will use the highest possible standard as a gauge in doing business and use the latest in technological innovations to make work more efficient, economical and systematic.

PROFESSIONAL JUICING SYSTEM


The Juice Master Professional

Unlike raw vegetables, plant cell walls are destroyed during the manufacturing process of raw juice and their various ingredients under cross-protection actions are exposed to the outside of cellular tissues. As a result, the various ingredients are easily changed through their easy contact with oxygen in the air. Especially when the most important ingredients of raw juice, beta carotene and Vitamin C are exposed to air, they have high possibility to be changed. In other words, when combined Vitamin C and mineral with oxygen, they are oxidized and destroyed or changed to insolubility. This means that they will lose all their effects.
