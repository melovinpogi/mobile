Who is NutriTECH

Since its founding in 2003, NutriTECH Alliance Corporation, a direct selling company has become the premier distributor of EXCLUSIVE Quality Home Living Premium products in the Philippines with a complete product line up that promotes healthy living. It is dedicated to increase the people's awareness on their health and well-being while attending to their basic needs, I.e., Food, Water and Air.

NutriTECH is a two word composition: NUTRItion and TECHnology hence promotes Good Nutrition through proper Food Preparation using the latest, up-to-date and innovative Technology where our products are made of. We offer high-quality, stylish products for every home, from the kitchen where family stays together.

As part of its advocacy to an excellent service, NutriTECH office is located at Suite 1901, the Centerpoint Building, Garnet Road, Pasig City. The company also owned a warehouse located in the City of Pasig with lodging facilities for out-of-town NutriTECHians. It also has a depo of products worth more than 250 million for storage. We have a transport vehicle for delivery and sales assistance.
